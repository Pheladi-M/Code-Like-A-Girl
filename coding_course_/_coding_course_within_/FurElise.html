Fur Elise

A love song which has imortalised dear Elise

Bagatelle No.25

Many rumours have swirlled regarding Beethoven but one this is for sure. Elise. She transcended all his accolades. All his struggles and Elise is what remians. A woman moved him beyond his human limitations and has hence created Fur Elise. A great work with which we are all familiar with and those of us who aren't, we have been given the opportunity to experience the song. Ambrosia for the ears. A story has been told without words but understood by everyone.



A shame they did not end up together.

A shame he was deaf

A shame he died in a thunderstorm

But a beauty he left behind
